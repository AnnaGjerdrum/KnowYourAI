Influence of the crowds

Privacy

There is a possibility that AI can influence people. By choosing which media people consume, which news they should follow and what they should listen to, you can sway peoples opinions in political matters or in economics. This can have major ramifications on society and democracy if not monitored.

In addition, AI is used in the stock market, where company shares are bought and sold. We expect a difference in company turnaround and popularity on the market if AI gets to freely trade without any time limit.
