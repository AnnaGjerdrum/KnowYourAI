What senses do we have?


Just like babies, AI have "senses" too. The senses available for an AI is limited to how it is implemented. A robot AI like this one for example have camera sensors to see, microphones to hear and tactile sensors to feel. They use these to obtain information about the world around them.

Keep in mind, not all AI have all these sensors, and can sometimes be very unaware of the world around them.
