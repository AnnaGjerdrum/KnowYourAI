What about our jobs!

What can we do then?

There are certain jobs AI will not be able to take over for a very long time. Empathy, creativity, human to human communication, human interaction etc.


The biggest importance with humans and AI is to learn to play each others strengths. Computers will never be experts on humans. AI's strengths are complex repetitive tasks wheras humans shine when you need to generalize, abstractify, adapt and think outside the box.


Empathic jobs are also difficult for AI to do, such as social work. Though efforts to make social robots for use in healthcare is at large, however far from approaching a humans ability to sympathize.
