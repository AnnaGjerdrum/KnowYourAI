Why is AI used for this?

Read about the different areas and see if you were correct!

Autonomous cars
While it is not yet fully developed, the field of autonomous cars have grown and we are now using AI to learn how to drive!
Social Robotics
Social robotics have grown as well, but the AI's embedded in these are no where near human intelligence and adaptability yet.
Recommender systems
Netflix, Amazon, Google and YouTube have used AI to help with their preduction and recommender algorithms that help figure out what the user might like to watch/buy.
AR/VR and Video games
AI technology is currently developed in these fields, but as of right now, its not that prominent yet.
Construction
It has yet to be used actively on a construction site, but AI is currently helping making calculations and assessing risks of building collapse.
Stock Market
AI has been developed to assess risk and execute stock exchange within seconds, increasing the amount of revenue gained from stock trade and allowing near-instant risk analysis
Medicine and Precise work
AI and ML technology use algorithms to analyze data and make predictions based on that information. AI programs can be at least 95% accurate on a regular basis. This is incredibly useful in the medical field when assessing risk of cancer or other hard to detect illnesses.
